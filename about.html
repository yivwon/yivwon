About Me

Hello! I'm Ivy, a product designer with a passion for creating intuitive and beautiful digital experiences. With a background in computer science, I bring a unique perspective to design challenges.

My journey in design began during my computer science studies when I realized that I was equally fascinated by how people interact with technology as I was with building it. This led me to explore the intersection of design and development, where I found my true calling.

I believe that great design is invisible—it should feel natural and intuitive to the user. My approach combines analytical thinking with creative problem-solving, allowing me to craft solutions that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new coffee shops, experimenting with creative coding, or adding to my collection of design books. I'm also a bubble tea enthusiast and am always on the lookout for the perfect brew.
